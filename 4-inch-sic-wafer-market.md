<p><p>The 4 Inch SiC Wafer market encompasses two main types: Conductive SiC Wafer and Semi-Insulating SiC Wafer. Conductive SiC wafers are engineered to have high electrical conductivity, making them suitable for applications requiring efficient heat dissipation and electrical conduction, such as power electronics. On the other hand, Semi-Insulating SiC wafers possess low electrical conductivity, enabling their utilization in high-frequency and high-power devices where minimal electrical interference is critical. Both types play a vital role in the development and implementation of advanced semiconductor technologies, enhancing performance and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
