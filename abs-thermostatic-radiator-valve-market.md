<p><p>ABS Thermostatic Radiator Valve market includes two types of valves: Self-Operated Temperature Control Valve and Electric Temperature Control Valve. The Self-Operated Temperature Control Valve is a type of valve that operates based on the temperature difference between the ambient temperature and set-point temperature. On the other hand, the Electric Temperature Control Valve utilizes an electric actuator to control the flow of water based on the temperature settings. Both types of valves are utilized in heating systems to maintain the desired temperature in a room or building.</p></p>
