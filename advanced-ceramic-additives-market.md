<p><ul><li>Dispersant</li><li>Binder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The advanced ceramic additives market consists of two main types: dispersants and binders. Dispersants are additives used to enhance the dispersion and stability of ceramic particles in a liquid medium, preventing agglomeration. They improve the flow of ceramic materials during processing and improve the overall quality of the final product. On the other hand, binders are substances that promote adhesion and cohesion of ceramic powders, allowing them to maintain their shape during drying and firing processes. This helps in achieving desired strength, shape, and integrity of ceramic products.</p></p>
