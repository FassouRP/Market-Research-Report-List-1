<p><p>Type I air-jacketed CO2 incubators are designed with a single system to heat the outer chamber and the air within it. This traditional design offers reliable temperature control but may experience some temperature gradients inside the chamber. In contrast, Type II air-jacketed CO2 incubators utilize an advanced heating system that provides uniform temperature throughout the entire chamber, eliminating temperature variations. The Type II model offers enhanced temperature stability, minimizing the risk of sample contamination and ensuring optimal growth conditions for cells.</p></p>
