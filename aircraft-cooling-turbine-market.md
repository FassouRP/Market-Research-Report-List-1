<p><p>The Aircraft Cooling Turbine market consists of two main types: Gas Cooling Turbine and Water Cooling Turbine. Gas Cooling Turbines utilize gases, such as air or exhaust, to provide cooling for aircraft components. These turbines are equipped with high-speed rotating blades that help extract heat. On the other hand, Water Cooling Turbines rely on the circulation and flow of water to remove heat from various aircraft systems. These turbines typically employ water jets or sprays to effectively cool down the components and ensure optimal performance.</p></p>
