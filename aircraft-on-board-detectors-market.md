<p><p>The Aircraft On-Board Detectors market consists of two types: Fixed Type and Portable Type. Fixed Type detectors are permanently installed in aircrafts and continuously monitor various parameters such as temperature, pressure, humidity, and contamination. These detectors provide real-time data and are essential for ensuring the safety and functionality of the aircraft. In contrast, Portable Type detectors are handheld devices that can be carried and used by personnel during inspections and maintenance tasks. These detectors offer flexibility and convenience, allowing for quick and targeted detection of abnormalities or issues in different aircraft systems.</p></p>
