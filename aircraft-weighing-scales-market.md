<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Aircraft weighing scales come in two types: platform and floor-standing. Platform scales consist of a flat surface where the aircraft is placed for weighing. They are usually portable and easy to move around for weighing smaller aircraft. Floor-standing scales, on the other hand, are larger and fixed to the ground. They are designed to support the weight of larger aircraft and provide a more stable and accurate weighing process. Both types of scales play a crucial role in ensuring the accurate measurement of an aircraft's weight for various safety and operational purposes.</p></p>
