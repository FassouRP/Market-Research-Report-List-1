<p><p>Alginate substitute is a gelatinous substance used in various industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and textiles. In the textile industry, alginate substitute is used as a sizing agent to improve the quality and durability of fabrics.</p><p>The alginate substitute market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
