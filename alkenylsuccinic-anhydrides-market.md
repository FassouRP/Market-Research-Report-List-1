<p><p>Alkenylsuccinic anhydrides (ASAs) are chemical compounds used in various industries. The market types for ASAs include OSA (Octenylsuccinic Anhydride), ODSA (Oleic Acid Succinic Anhydride), NSA (Nonylsuccinic Anhydride), DDSA (Dodecenylsuccinic Anhydride), and others. OSA is derived from octenylsuccinic acid and is widely used as emulsifiers in food and personal care products. ODSA is synthesized from oleic acid and finds applications in adhesives and coatings. NSA and DDSA are derived from nonyl and dodecenyl acids respectively, and are used in surfactants and lubricants. These ASAs serve various purposes based on their unique chemical properties.</p></p>
