<p><p>Alumina Ceramic Film is a type of material used in various filtration processes. Microfiltration Ceramic Film is a subtype, offering precise filtration by trapping particles larger than its pore size. This enables the removal of impurities from liquids or gases. Ultrafiltration Ceramic Film, on the other hand, allows even finer filtration by separating molecules based on size and molecular weight. This type of ceramic film is commonly used in applications requiring high purity, such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing. Both types offer reliable filtration solutions for different filtration needs.</p></p>
