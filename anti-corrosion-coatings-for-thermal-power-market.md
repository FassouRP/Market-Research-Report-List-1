<p><p>Anti-corrosion coatings are essential in the thermal power market to protect equipment and structures from corrosion damage. There are three main types of coatings: solvent-based, water-based, and powder coatings. Solvent-based coatings are composed of organic solvents, providing excellent adhesion and resistance to corrosion. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly alternatives, utilizing water as a carrier with low toxicity levels. Powder coatings are made of finely ground particles that are electrostatically applied and cured to form a protective layer. Each type has its advantages and is selected based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
