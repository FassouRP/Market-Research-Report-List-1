<p><p>The Anti-Static ABS Resin market consists of three types: Permanent Anti-Static Type, Static Dissipative Type, and Conductive Type. The Permanent Anti-Static Type has a built-in anti-static property that lasts throughout the product's lifecycle. The Static Dissipative Type allows static electricity to dissipate gradually, minimizing the risk of damage or spark formation. The Conductive Type, on the other hand, conducts electricity, effectively eliminating static charges. These different types cater to specific industry needs, providing options for preventing static-related issues in various applications.</p></p>
