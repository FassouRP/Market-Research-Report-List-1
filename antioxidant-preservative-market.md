Erythorbic acid is a chemical compound used to preserve the color and flavor of food products. Propyl gallate is another synthetic preservative that prevents the oxidation of fats and oils. Tocopherols, including vitamin E, are commonly used antioxidants that help extend the shelf life of products by inhibiting the growth of bacteria and fungi.</p></p>
