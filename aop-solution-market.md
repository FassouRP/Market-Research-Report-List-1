<p><p>The AOP solution market encompasses various types of technologies used for advanced oxidation processes. Photochemical AOPs utilize ultraviolet (UV) light to generate hydroxyl radicals and oxidize pollutants. Catalytic Wet AOPs involve the use of catalysts to accelerate oxidation reactions in the presence of water. Sonochemical AOPs use high-intensity ultrasound waves to generate cavitation and promote chemical reactions. Ozone AOPs rely on ozone gas to oxidize contaminants. Electrochemical AOPs employ an electric current to generate reactive species that degrade pollutants. Fenton AOPs involve the use of iron catalysts and hydrogen peroxide to generate hydroxyl radicals for oxidation.</p></p>
