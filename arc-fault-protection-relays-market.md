<p><p>Arc fault protection relays are electrical devices designed to detect and respond to arc faults in electrical systems. These relays provide an additional layer of safety by continuously monitoring electrical currents and interrupting the power supply if an arc fault is detected. These faults can lead to electrical fires and pose a significant safety risk.</p><p>The future outlook for the arc fault protection relays market is promising, driven by factors such as increasing emphasis on electrical safety, regulatory requirements, and the growing adoption of arc fault detection technology.
