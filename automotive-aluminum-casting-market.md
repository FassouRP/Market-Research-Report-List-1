<p><p>The automotive aluminum casting market includes various types of casting methods. Die casting is a process where molten aluminum is injected into a metal mold under high pressure to produce complex shapes. Permanent mold casting involves pouring molten aluminum into a reusable mold made of steel or iron. Sand casting uses a mixture of sand and a binder to create molds, which are then filled with molten aluminum. Other casting methods in this market may include investment casting or centrifugal casting, each offering distinct advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
