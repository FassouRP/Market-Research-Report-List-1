<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive body sealing systems market consists of three main types: EPDM sealing system, PVC sealing system, and TPE sealing system. The EPDM sealing system utilizes ethylene propylene diene monomer rubber, known for its excellent weather resistance and durability. PVC sealing systems use polyvinyl chloride, which offers good chemical resistance but limited temperature tolerance. TPE sealing systems employ thermoplastic elastomers, combining the characteristics of both rubber and plastic for greater flexibility and cost-effectiveness. These systems play a crucial role in preventing water, dust, and noise infiltration in automobiles.</p></p>
