<p><ul><li>Compensating</li><li>Semi-oscillating</li><li>Fully Oscillating</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Automotive fifth wheel couplings refer to the mechanism used to connect the tractor and trailer of a heavy-duty vehicle. In the compensating type, the coupling compensates for the lateral forces encountered during cornering. The semi-oscillating type allows limited rotational movement between the tractor and trailer, enhancing maneuverability. On the other hand, the fully oscillating type provides complete freedom of movement and helps maintain stability on uneven terrain. These different types cater to specific requirements, providing various levels of flexibility and stability during the operation of heavy-duty vehicles.</p></p>
