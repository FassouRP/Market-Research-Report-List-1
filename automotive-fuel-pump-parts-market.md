Driving shaft thrust bearing supports the driving shaft, reducing friction and ensuring smooth operation. Transmission gear transmits power from the driving shaft to the driven gear. Other components in this market include seals, valves, and diaphragms that aid in the functioning of the fuel pump.</p></p>
