<p><p>The Automotive King Pin Market is categorized into two main types: Pressing Bushing Type and Floating Bushing Type. The Pressing Bushing Type involves the use of a fixed bushing that is pressed into the steering knuckle, which restricts the movement of the king pin. On the other hand, the Floating Bushing Type allows for the king pin to move freely within the bushing, enhancing flexibility in steering. These different market types cater to the diverse needs and preferences of automotive manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
