<p><ul><li>OEMs</li><li>Aftermarket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive launch control systems are classified into two main market types: Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) and Aftermarket. OEMs refer to launch control systems that are installed by the vehicle manufacturer during the production process. These systems are integrated into the car's electronic control unit and are designed specifically for that particular vehicle model. On the other hand, Aftermarket launch control systems are not factory-installed but rather are purchased and installed separately by car owners or enthusiasts. These systems are often pre-programmed or customizable, offering enhanced performance and control during vehicle launches.</p></p>
