<p><ul><li>Simple Planetary Gear</li><li>Complex Planetary Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Planetary Gear market is categorized into two types: Simple Planetary Gear and Complex Planetary Gear. Simple Planetary Gear consists of one central gear (sun gear) surrounded by multiple gears (planet gears) and encased in an outer ring gear (ring gear). It provides basic gear functions like torque conversion and speed reduction. On the other hand, Complex Planetary Gear combines the same components as a simple gear but also includes additional elements such as clutches, brakes, and multiple sun gears, allowing for advanced functionalities like gear shifting and speed control.</p></p>
