<p><p>The automotive tires market consists of two main types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires are constructed with overlapping layers of fabric cords in a crisscross pattern, which provides strength and flexibility. They have a simple construction and are durable, making them suitable for off-road and heavy-duty vehicles. On the other hand, radial tires have fabric cords arranged in a radial direction from the tire's center to the outer edges, offering better traction, fuel efficiency, and a comfortable ride. Radial tires are commonly used in passenger cars and commercial vehicles.</p></p>
