<p><p>Baggage Transport and Sortation Systems are used in airports to efficiently transport and sort luggage. There are two main types in the market - RFID and Barcode systems. RFID systems use radio frequency identification technology to track and identify luggage, allowing for automated sorting and easy retrieval. Barcode systems use barcode labels to track and sort luggage, relying on scanners for identification. Both systems aim to enhance baggage handling operations, reduce errors, and improve overall efficiency in airports.</p></p>
