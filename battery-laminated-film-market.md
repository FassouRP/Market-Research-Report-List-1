<p><ul><li>Thermal Process</li><li>Dry Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Battery laminated film is used in the production of lithium-ion batteries. There are two types of processes used in the manufacturing of this film - thermal process and dry process. </p><p>The thermal process involves heat and pressure to create a laminated film by melting and bonding multiple layers of polymer films. This process is widely used due to its ability to produce a uniform and tightly bonded film.</p><p>The dry process, on the other hand, does not involve heat or pressure. Instead, it uses adhesives or solvent-based methods to bond the layers of polymer films together. This process is often chosen for its simplicity and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>Both types of processes play a crucial role in the battery laminated film market, providing different options for manufacturers depending on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
