<p><p>The benchtop total organic carbon analyzer (TOC) market comprises three main types: solid TOC analyzer, liquid TOC analyzer, and solid-liquid integrated analyzer. Solid TOC analyzers analyze the organic carbon content in solid samples, such as soil or sediments. Liquid TOC analyzers measure organic carbon in liquid samples, like wastewater or drinking water. Solid-liquid integrated analyzers can analyze both solid and liquid samples, providing a versatile solution for various applications. These analyzers are widely used in environmental monitoring, water quality assessment, and industrial research.</p></p>
