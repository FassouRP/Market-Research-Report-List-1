<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Bioimpedance measuring devices are used to measure electrical impedance in biological tissues, which can provide information about a person's body composition. Single-frequency bioimpedance devices send a single electrical frequency through the body and measure the impedance that results, providing basic body composition data. Multiple-frequency bioimpedance devices use multiple electrical frequencies to analyze different tissue types, offering more detailed information about body composition, such as fat mass, total body water, and muscle mass. Both types of devices are used in various healthcare settings for assessing patient health and fitness levels.</p></p>
