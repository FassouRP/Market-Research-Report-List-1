<p><strong>The Boiler Automation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensate Contamination Control System</li><li>Automatic Blowdown Systems</li><li>Level Control Systems</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boiler Automation Systems market encompasses various types of systems that aim to enhance the efficiency and reliability of boiler operations. One type is the Condensate Contamination Control System, which prevents the entry of impurities into the condensate system, ensuring its purity. Another type is the Automatic Blowdown System, which eliminates impurities and maintains the boiler's water quality by regularly discharging a portion of the boiler water. Level Control Systems maintain the boiler's water level within a safe range. Lastly, the "Others" market comprises additional systems such as flame scanner systems and soot blowers that assist in boiler automation.</p></p>
