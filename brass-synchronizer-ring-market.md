<p><strong>The Brass Synchronizer Ring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Cone Ring</li><li>Multi Cone Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brass synchronizer ring market is divided into two types: single cone ring and multi cone ring. Single cone rings are characterized by a single set of external splines and internal teeth that engage with the hub teeth and gear, respectively. On the other hand, multi cone rings consist of multiple sets of external splines and internal teeth, allowing different gears in the transmission to engage simultaneously with the hub teeth. These two types cater to different transmission systems and offer varying levels of gear engagement.</p></p>
