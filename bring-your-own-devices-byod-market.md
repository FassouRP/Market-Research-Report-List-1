<p><p>Bring Your Own Devices (BYOD) refers to the policy in which organizations allow employees to use their personal mobile devices, such as smartphones, tablets, and laptops, for work-related tasks. Moreover, the COVID-19 pandemic has accelerated the adoption of BYOD, as remote work became the new normal for many organizations. As a result, the BYOD market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years as more companies recognize the benefits of this approach and implement appropriate policies and security measures.</p></p>
