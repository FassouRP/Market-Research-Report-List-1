<p><p>Burst discs are pressure relief devices used in various industries to protect equipment from overpressure situations. There are different types available in the market. Positive arch burst discs are designed to have a convex shape that allows for higher burst pressures. Anti-arch burst discs, on the other hand, have a concave shape and are generally used in applications where lower burst pressures are required. Flat type burst discs have a flat surface and are suitable for applications with moderate burst pressures. Apart from these three types, there are other variations available that cater to different industry-specific needs.</p></p>
