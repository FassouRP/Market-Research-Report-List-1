<p><strong>The Bus Black Box Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated</li><li>Portable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bus Black Box market is divided into two types: Integrated and Portable. In the Integrated market, the black box system is embedded directly into the bus's infrastructure. This provides seamless integration with other vehicle systems, enabling real-time monitoring and analysis of various parameters such as speed, location, and engine performance. On the other hand, Portable black box systems are standalone devices that can be easily installed in different buses. They offer flexibility and convenience as they can be transferred across multiple vehicles, allowing fleet owners to monitor their buses' performance and ensure safety without the need for extensive installations.</p></p>
