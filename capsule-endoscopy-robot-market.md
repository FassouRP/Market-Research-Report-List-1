<p><p>The capsule endoscopy robot market comprises two main types: internally driven type and magnetron type. Internally driven robots are equipped with small internal motors that enable movement and navigation through the gastrointestinal tract. They provide clinicians with real-time imaging and diagnostic capabilities. On the other hand, magnetron robots utilize external magnetic forces to propel and steer the capsule within the body. This type offers improved control and maneuverability compared to the internally driven robots. Both types contribute to advancements in non-invasive medical procedures and enhance diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
