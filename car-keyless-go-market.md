<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Access (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Access (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car keyless go market includes two main types of technology: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allows for effortless access to a vehicle without the need to physically press any buttons on the key fob. It operates based on proximity, automatically unlocking the vehicle as the driver approaches. On the other hand, RKES requires the driver to press a button on the key fob to unlock or lock the car remotely. Both systems offer convenience and enhanced security features for car owners.</p></p>
