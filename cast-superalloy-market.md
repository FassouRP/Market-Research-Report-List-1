<p><p>Cast Superalloy market types include iron base, nickel base, cobalt base, and other types. Iron base cast superalloys are primarily composed of iron and have good mechanical properties and high strength at high temperatures. Nickel base cast superalloys are made up of nickel and have excellent oxidation and corrosion resistance, along with good creep resistance. Cobalt base cast superalloys contain cobalt and are known for their superior high-temperature strength and resistance to thermal fatigue. The "Other" category encompasses superalloys that are not classified under the aforementioned types, which may include alloys based on elements like titanium, tungsten, and molybdenum.</p></p>
