<p><p>The Cationic Dyeable Polyester Chip (CDP) market refers to the segment of the polyester chip market that involves the production and sale of polyester chips suitable for cationic dyeing. On the other hand, the Exhaustive Cationic Dyeable Polyester Chip (ECDP) market focuses on offering polyester chips that can be exhaustively dyed using cationic dyes, ensuring even and consistent coloration throughout the fabric. Both CDP and ECDP cater to the demand for polyester chips suitable for cationic dyeing processes.</p></p>
