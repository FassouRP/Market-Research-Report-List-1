Onium Salt Photoinitiators, Metal-organic Photoinitiators, and Organosilane Photoinitiators are the three main types of cationic photoinitiators found in the market. Onium Salt Photoinitiators are compounds containing onium salts as the initiating group, Metal-organic Photoinitiators consist of metal complexes, and Organosilane Photoinitiators include silane-based compounds.
