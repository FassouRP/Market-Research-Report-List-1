<p><strong>The Cheese Coagulation Detection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cheese coagulation detection systems refer to the equipment used to monitor and control the process of curd formation in cheese making. Contact detection systems involve direct physical contact with the curd to determine its coagulation state, usually through probe sensors or devices. Non-contact detection systems, on the other hand, utilize technologies like infrared or ultrasonic sensors to assess the coagulation process without physically touching the curd. Both types aim to ensure the proper timing and optimize the quality of cheese production through accurate detection of coagulation.</p></p>
