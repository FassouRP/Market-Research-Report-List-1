<p><ul><li>Alkaline Degreaser</li><li>Emulsion Degreaser</li><li>Solvent Degreaser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical degreaser market can be categorized into three main types: alkaline degreasers, emulsion degreasers, and solvent degreasers. Alkaline degreasers are water-based and effective in removing grease and oil from various surfaces. Emulsion degreasers are oil-in-water mixtures and are commonly used for heavy-duty cleaning tasks. Solvent degreasers, on the other hand, are based on various solvents and are particularly efficient in dissolving and removing heavy grease and oil deposits. These different types cater to various industrial cleaning needs, providing diverse options for degreasing applications.</p></p>
