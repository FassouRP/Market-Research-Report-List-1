<p><p>Chemicals for the swimming pool market can be categorized into different types. Beaching powder, also known as calcium hypochlorite, is a popular choice as it effectively disinfects the pool water. Sodium hypochlorite, commonly known as liquid bleach, is another option that helps maintain water hygiene. Liquid chlorine is highly concentrated and a powerful sanitizer used in larger pools. Trichloroisocyanuric acid (TCCA) is a slow-dissolving chlorine tablet that provides long-lasting sanitation. Additionally, there are other chemicals available in the market that cater to specific pool needs, such as pH adjusters, algaecides, and flocculants.</p></p>
