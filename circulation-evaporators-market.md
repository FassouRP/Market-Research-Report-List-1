<p><p>Circulation evaporators are a type of heat exchange system used in various industries to remove water or other solvents from a liquid mixture. Natural circulation evaporators rely on gravity and temperature differences to circulate the liquid, while forced circulation evaporators use pumps to circulate the liquid. Natural circulation evaporators are typically used for low to medium concentration solutions, while forced circulation evaporators are used for high concentration solutions. Both types provide efficient and cost-effective solutions for evaporating liquids in industrial processes.</p></p>
