<p><ul><li>Automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the citrus processing device market, there are two main types of devices available: automatic and semi-automatic. </p><p>Automatic citrus processing devices are fully automated and require minimal human intervention. They perform all the processing tasks, such as peeling, juicing, and separating the pulp from the juice, without the need for manual assistance. These devices are highly efficient and suitable for large-scale citrus processing operations.</p><p>Semi-automatic citrus processing devices, on the other hand, require some level of human involvement. While they automate certain processing tasks, such as peeling or juicing, they still require manual loading and unloading of citrus fruits. These devices are often used in smaller-scale or artisanal citrus processing operations.</p></p>
