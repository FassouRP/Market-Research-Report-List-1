<p><strong>The Cleanroom Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cleanroom monitoring systems are used in controlled environments to ensure the cleanliness and safety of the area. The market for these systems can be categorized into two types: automatic and semi-automatic. </p><p>Automatic cleanroom monitoring systems are fully automated and equipped with advanced sensors and software that continuously monitor various parameters such as temperature, humidity, particle count, and air pressure. These systems provide real-time data and can send alerts or take corrective actions automatically when parameters deviate from the set limits.</p><p>On the other hand, semi-automatic cleanroom monitoring systems require some manual intervention. They utilize sensors to measure parameters but may not have automated alert systems. Instead, operators need to manually review the data and take necessary actions.</p><p>Both types of systems play a crucial role in maintaining the cleanliness and integrity of cleanrooms, but automatic systems offer higher levels of efficiency and real-time monitoring capabilities compared to semi-automatic ones.</p></p>
