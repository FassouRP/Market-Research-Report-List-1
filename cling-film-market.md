<p><p>Cling film, also known as plastic wrap, comes in various types. Polyethylene is the most common type, valued for its low cost and versatility. Biaxially Oriented PolyPropylene (BOPP) cling film offers higher clarity and strength, making it suitable for food packaging. PVC cling film is highly flexible and provides excellent cling, but is not microwave-safe. PVDC cling film offers exceptional barrier properties and is commonly used for wrapping and freezing foods. "Others" category includes cling films made from different materials, such as biodegradable or compostable options.</p></p>
