<p><p>Co-Processed Excipients Market Types involve various methods such as granulation, spray drying, hot melt extrusion, solvent evaporation, and others. Granulation combines active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) with excipients to form granules for easy dosage forms. Spray drying involves transforming liquid excipients into a fine powder through the application of heat. Hot melt extrusion enables mixing APIs and excipients at high temperatures to create a uniform extrudate. Solvent evaporation includes dissolving APIs and excipients in a solvent, which is later evaporated, leaving behind a solid product. Other methods involve different techniques like co-melting or solid-state mixing to form co-processed excipients.</p></p>
