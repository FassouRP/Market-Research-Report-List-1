<p><p>The commercial aviation sensors and switches market encompasses various types of sensors and switches used in aviation applications. Proximity sensors detect the presence or absence of an object within a defined range. Position sensors measure the linear or angular position of components. Speed sensors monitor the speed of rotating parts. Thermal sensors measure temperature. Pressure sensors detect fluid or gas pressure. Field switches control electrical circuits. Other types include humidity sensors, flow sensors, and acceleration sensors, among others. These components play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient operation of commercial aviation systems.</p></p>
