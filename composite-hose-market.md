<p><p>Composite hose is a flexible and lightweight hose made from multiple layers of materials such as polypropylene, polyethylene, and thermoplastic films. It is specifically designed to handle a wide range of aggressive chemicals, fuels, and gases. The hose consists of an inner wire helix for reinforcement, surrounded by multiple layers of thermoplastic films, and an outer wire helix for additional strength and stability. The unique construction of composite hose ensures resistance against corrosion, abrasion, and chemical attacks, making it a preferred choice for various industries including chemical, petroleum, food and beverage, and pharmaceutical.</p><p>The future outlook for the composite hose market is promising.
