<p><p>The Critical Care Therapeutic Bed market includes two main types: Mechanical Type and Electric Type. Mechanical Type beds are manually adjustable and typically operated through a crank or lever system. They offer basic functionalities and are less expensive compared to Electric Type beds. Electric Type beds, on the other hand, are motorized and capable of various adjustments through remote controls. They provide advanced features such as height adjustments, side rail movements, and patient positioning aids. Electric Type beds are more expensive but offer enhanced convenience and comfort for patients and caregivers.</p></p>
