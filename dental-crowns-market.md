<p><p>Dental crowns are commonly used to restore damaged teeth. There are different types of dental crowns available in the market, including porcelain-fused-to-metal (PFM), all-ceramics, and metal crowns. </p><p>PFM crowns are made by placing a porcelain layer over a metal base, providing strength and aesthetic appeal. All-ceramic crowns are entirely composed of ceramic materials, providing excellent aesthetics and biocompatibility. Metal crowns, on the other hand, are made of alloys like gold or nickel-chromium, known for their durability and strength. Each type of crown has its advantages and suitability depending on a patient's specific dental needs.</p></p>
