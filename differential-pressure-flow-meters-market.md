<p><p>Differential pressure flow meters are categorized into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I meters are traditional flow meters that measure the differential pressure between two points in a flow system. They are commonly used for simple and low-cost applications. On the other hand, Type II meters, also known as inferential meters, calculate the flow rate by using additional variables like fluid density and temperature. These meters are more accurate and versatile, making them suitable for complex and high-precision applications.</p></p>
