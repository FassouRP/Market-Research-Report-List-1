<p><p>Flexo printing, digital printing, and offset printing are different types of printing methods used in the direct thermal linerless labels market. </p><p>Flexo printing is a traditional printing method that uses a flexible relief plate to transfer ink onto the labels. It is a popular choice for large volume production and offers good color vibrancy and durability.</p><p>Digital printing is a modern printing method that directly prints images onto the labels using digital files. It allows for quick turnarounds, customization, and minimal setup costs.</p><p>Offset printing involves transferring ink from a plate to a rubber blanket and then onto the labels. It is suitable for high-quality printing and can handle both small and large print runs.</p></p>
