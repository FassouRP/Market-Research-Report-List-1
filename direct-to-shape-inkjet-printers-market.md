<p><p>Direct-to-Shape Inkjet Printers are categorized based on the types of inks used in the printing process. Solvent-based inks are more durable and are suitable for printing on non-porous materials like plastics and metals. UV curing inks use ultraviolet radiation to instantly dry and cure the ink, making them ideal for printing on various substrates.
