<p><p>The drilling fluid additives market comprises various types of additives used in drilling operations. Fluid viscosifiers are chemicals that increase the viscosity of the drilling fluid, improving its ability to suspend cuttings and maintain hole stability. Corrosion inhibitors protect the drilling equipment from rust and degradation. Dispersants prevent clumping of solids in the drilling fluid. Surface modifiers enhance the wettability of solid surfaces. Defoamers eliminate foam formation in the fluid. Biocides help control microbial growth. All these additives play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and safe drilling operations.</p></p>
