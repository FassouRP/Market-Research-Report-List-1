<p><p>The elastomer bearing market encompasses various types of bearings, such as neoprene bearing and laminated elastic bearing. Neoprene bearings are made of a synthetic rubber called neoprene, known for its excellent resistance to weathering, chemicals, and ozone. These bearings are used in various applications, including bridges, buildings, and heavy machinery, to absorb shock, dampen vibration, and allow movement. On the other hand, laminated elastic bearings consist of multiple layers of rubber and steel, offering high load capacity, flexibility, and durability, often utilized in structures with significant vertical movement or rotation.</p></p>
