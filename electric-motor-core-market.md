The increasing adoption of energy-efficient electric motors across various industries is one of the key factors driving the market growth. Electric motors are a crucial component in EVs, and as the demand for EVs continues to rise, the demand for electric motor cores is also expected to witness substantial growth.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in motor core materials and designs are further fueling the market growth.
