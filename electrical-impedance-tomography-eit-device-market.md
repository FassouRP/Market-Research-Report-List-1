<p><p>The Electrical Impedance Tomography (EIT) device market consists of two main types: Static EIT and Dynamic EIT. Static EIT involves measuring and imaging electrical impedance values at a particular point in time, providing a snapshot of the patient's condition. On the other hand, Dynamic EIT captures real-time changes in impedance over time, allowing for continuous monitoring and visualization of changes within the body. Both types offer valuable insights for medical professionals in diagnosing and managing various conditions.</p></p>
