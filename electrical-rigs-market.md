<p><strong>The Electrical RIGs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class1 and Class2</li><li>Class3 and Class4</li><li>Class0 and Class00</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical RIGs are categorized into different classes based on their level of electrical insulation and safety. Class 1 and Class 2 RIGs are designed for general purpose use, with Class 1 offering basic insulation and Class 2 incorporating double insulation for enhanced shock protection. Class 3 and Class 4 RIGs are specialized equipment used in high-risk environments, with Class 3 providing extra-low voltage (ELV) protection and Class 4 ensuring insulation against electric shocks. Lastly, Class 0 and Class 00 RIGs are specifically designed for working with sensitive electronic devices, with Class 0 offering maximum protection against electrical disturbances and Class 00 providing additional protection for static sensitive components.</p></p>
