<p><p>The electrode spark plug market is divided into two types: hot spark plugs and cold spark plugs. Hot spark plugs are designed to operate at higher temperatures and are typically used in engines that run at high speeds or under heavy loads. On the other hand, cold spark plugs are designed for engines that operate at lower temperatures or with less intense conditions. The choice between hot and cold spark plugs depends on the specific engine requirements and is determined by factors such as engine design, fuel type, and operating conditions.</p></p>
