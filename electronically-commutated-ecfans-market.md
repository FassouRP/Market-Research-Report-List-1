<p><p>The Electronically Commutated (EC) Fans market comprises various types of fans, namely Centrifugal Fans, Axial Fans, Mixed Flow Fans, and Cross Flow Fans. Centrifugal fans are widely used in applications that require high-pressure airflow, such as HVAC systems in buildings. Axial fans are the most common type, known for their ability to move large volumes of air with low pressure. Mixed flow fans combine features of both centrifugal and axial fans, making them suitable for applications that require a balance between airflow and pressure. Cross flow fans are commonly used in HVAC systems, where they provide a laminar airflow across a large surface area.</p></p>
