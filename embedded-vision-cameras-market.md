<p><p>The Embedded Vision Cameras market consists of two primary types: Area Scan Camera and Line Scan Camera. Area Scan Cameras capture an entire image of a scene, similar to how a regular digital camera works. They are suitable for applications that require capturing still or moving objects. On the other hand, Line Scan Cameras capture images line by line, enabling analysis of continuous objects or surfaces such as in printing or material inspection industries. Both camera types play integral roles in various industries for their distinct imaging capabilities.</p></p>
