<p><p>Engineered molded wood pallets are a type of pallet made from compressed wood fibers. There are two main types: nestable pallets and rackable pallets. Nestable pallets have a design that allows them to stack neatly within each other, minimizing storage space when not in use. They are commonly used for one-way shipping or lightweight loads. Rackable pallets, on the other hand, are designed to be more durable and able to withstand heavy loads in racking systems. They are often used for storage purposes and can be stacked on top of each other safely.</p></p>
