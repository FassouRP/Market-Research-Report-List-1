<p><p>An EV Battery Thermal Management System aims to regulate the temperature of electric vehicle (EV) batteries to ensure optimal performance, longevity, and safety. The two main types of systems are Active Management System and Passive Management System. </p><p>An Active Management System actively controls the battery temperature by using cooling or heating mechanisms, such as a liquid-based cooling system or a heat pump. </p><p>On the other hand, a Passive Management System relies on natural heat dissipation or insulation to maintain the battery temperature, without the necessity for active cooling or heating components.</p></p>
