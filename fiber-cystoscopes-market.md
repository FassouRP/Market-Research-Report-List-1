<p><p>Type I fiber cystoscopes are flexible cystoscopes that are used for diagnostic purposes. Type II fiber cystoscopes, on the other hand, are rigid cystoscopes that are used for both diagnostic and therapeutic procedures. They are larger in diameter and allow for the removal of tissue samples, stone fragmentation, and various other treatments in addition to diagnostic examinations. Both types of fiber cystoscopes are important tools in urological examinations and procedures.</p></p>
