<p><p>The floor conveyor system market comprises various types such as belt conveyors, slat conveyors, bucket elevators, telescopic conveyors, towline conveyors, gravity conveyors, powered roller conveyors, and turnkey floor conveyors. Belt conveyors use a continuous loop of material to transport items. Slat conveyors have slats that move items along. Bucket elevators use buckets to vertically transport bulk materials. Telescopic conveyors extend or retract to adjust length. Towline conveyors use carts or carriers pulled along a fixed path. Gravity conveyors use the force of gravity to move items. Powered roller conveyors use motorized rollers to transport items. Turnkey floor conveyors are complete conveyor systems offered as a packaged solution.</p></p>
