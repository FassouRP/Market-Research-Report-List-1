<p><p>Fumed alumina is a nanoparticle synthesized through flame pyrolysis of aluminum chlorides. It finds extensive application in various industries due to its unique properties. The market for fumed alumina can be categorized into two main types: hydrophobic and hydrophilic. Hydrophobic fumed alumina possesses water-repellent properties, making it suitable for applications requiring moisture resistance, such as coatings and sealants. On the other hand, hydrophilic fumed alumina has moisture-absorbing characteristics, making it ideal for applications involving water-based systems, such as pharmaceuticals and catalysts. These two types cater to different industry needs and offer versatile solutions based on their respective properties.</p></p>
