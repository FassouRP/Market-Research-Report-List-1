<p><p>Grinding and polishing abrasive market consists of three main types: bonded abrasives, coated abrasives, and super abrasives. Bonded abrasives are made by mixing abrasive grains with bonding materials, forming them into wheels or shapes. Coated abrasives have abrasive grains coated onto a backing material, such as sandpaper. Super abrasives are advanced materials, like diamond or cubic boron nitride, that are used for precision grinding and polishing applications. These three types serve different purposes and have distinct characteristics, catering to various industries and their specific needs.</p></p>
