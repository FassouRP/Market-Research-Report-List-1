<p><strong>The Guided Airflow System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mixing Ventilation</li><li>Parallel Flow Ventilation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Guided Airflow System market consists of two main types: Mixing Ventilation and Parallel Flow Ventilation. Mixing Ventilation involves the mixing of supply and exhaust air within a space, providing a uniform temperature and air quality throughout. Parallel Flow Ventilation involves the separation of supply and exhaust air streams, maintaining a clear distinction between conditioned and non-conditioned zones. Both systems aim to improve indoor air quality and thermal comfort, but each uses different methods to achieve these goals.</p></p>
