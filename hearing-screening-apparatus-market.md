<p><p>Hearing screening apparatus market types include OAE (Otoacoustic Emissions), ABR (Auditory Brainstem Response), and Other market segments. OAE involves measuring the sounds emitted by the inner ear in response to external stimuli, providing information about the cochlear function. ABR measures the electrical activity generated by the auditory nerve and brainstem in response to sound, aiding in the assessment of hearing loss. The Other market segment likely includes alternative or less common methods of hearing screening, such as psychoacoustic tests or digitally-based assessments.</p></p>
