<p><p>The high capacity linear actuator market consists of two main types: pneumatic and electric. Pneumatic linear actuators use compressed air to create linear motion, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications that require a high force output. On the other hand, electric linear actuators utilize electric motors to generate linear motion, providing precise control and accurate positioning. Both types have their advantages, with pneumatic actuators offering robustness and electric actuators offering versatility and precision. The choice between the two depends on specific application requirements and preferences.</p></p>
