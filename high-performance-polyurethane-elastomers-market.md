<p><p>High Performance Polyurethane Elastomers are a type of material used in various industries for their exceptional performance characteristics. The market can be categorized into two types: Thermoset PU Elastomers and Thermoplastic PU Elastomers. Thermoset PU Elastomers are crosslinked polymers that cannot be melted or reshaped once cured, providing excellent durability and resistance to heat, chemicals, and abrasion. In contrast, Thermoplastic PU Elastomers can be melted and reshaped multiple times without losing their properties, offering versatility, flexibility, and ease of processing.</p></p>
