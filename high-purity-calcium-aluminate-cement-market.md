<p><p>High purity calcium aluminate cement (CAC) is a specialized cementitious material composed of calcium aluminate phases with a high alumina content. High purity CAC exhibits excellent high-temperature properties, rapid setting, and quick strength development.</p><p>The high purity calcium aluminate cement market is projected to experience substantial growth during the forecast period, with a CAGR of 13.8%. The market is primarily driven by the increasing demand for high-performance refractory materials, especially in industries such as steel, cement, and glass. High purity CAC finds extensive applications in various heat-resistant products, such as refractory castables, mortars, and gunning mixes, due to its exceptional thermal stability and strength.</p><p>Additionally, the growth of the construction sector, particularly in emerging economies, is expected to contribute to the market expansion.
