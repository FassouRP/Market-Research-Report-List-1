<p><p>High temperature vacuum grease is available in two main types: silicone based and PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) based. Silicone-based grease offers high stability and thermal resistance, making it suitable for extreme temperature applications. It is often used in vacuum systems, o-rings, and lubrication of control valves. On the other hand, PTFE-based grease offers excellent chemical resistance and low friction properties. It is commonly used in equipment that operates under high temperature and high vacuum conditions, such as aerospace components and high-performance industrial machinery.</p></p>
