<p><ul><li>Microfiltration Hollow Fiber Filter</li><li>Ultrafiltration Hollow Fiber Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hollow Fiber Filter Market includes two main types: Microfiltration Hollow Fiber Filters and Ultrafiltration Hollow Fiber Filters. Microfiltration Hollow Fiber Filters are designed to remove particulate matter and microorganisms from a liquid by passing it through a membrane with small pore size. On the other hand, Ultrafiltration Hollow Fiber Filters have a smaller pore size and are capable of removing not only particles but also molecules such as proteins and viruses. Both types of filters are widely used in industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, and biotechnology.</p></p>
