<p><p>Hot-work die steels are a crucial component in various industrial processes. The market is categorized into three types: hammer forging die, hot extrusion die, and die-casting die. Hammer forging die involves shaping metal using repeated blows, while hot extrusion die facilitates the creation of complex shapes by forcing the metal through a die. Die-casting die is used in the production of intricate metal parts using high-pressure injection. Each of these market segments plays a significant role in different manufacturing applications, contributing to the overall growth of the hot-work die steels market.</p></p>
