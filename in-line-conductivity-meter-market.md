<p><p>The in-line conductivity meter market consists of two main types: single in-line conductivity meters and dual in-line conductivity meters. Single in-line conductivity meters are designed to measure electrical conductivity in a single stream or flow of liquid. They are typically used in applications where only one liquid is being measured. On the other hand, dual in-line conductivity meters are capable of measuring conductivity in two separate streams simultaneously. This type of meter is commonly used in situations where multiple liquids need to be monitored or mixed accurately.</p></p>
