<p><ul><li>Single Screw</li><li>Twin Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial extruders are machines used in various industries to process and shape materials. The single screw market refers to the market for industrial extruders that have a single rotating screw inside the barrel, which is responsible for both melting and pushing the material through the machine. On the other hand, the twin screw market involves industrial extruders that have two intermeshing screws inside the barrel. Twin screw extruders are capable of providing enhanced mixing, higher throughput, and are able to process a wider range of materials compared to single screw extruders.</p></p>
