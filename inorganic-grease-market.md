<p><p>The inorganic grease market consists of various types, with two prominent ones being bentonite grease and silicone grease. Bentonite grease is a type of inorganic grease that is made by incorporating bentonite clay into a base oil and thickener. It offers excellent high-temperature stability and good water resistance. Silicone grease, on the other hand, is made using silicone oil and a thickener, offering excellent thermal stability, water resistance, and electrical insulation properties. Both types find applications in diverse industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics, due to their unique characteristics.</p></p>
