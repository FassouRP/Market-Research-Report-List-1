<p><p>Interior wall coatings can be categorized into three types: water-soluble coatings, solvent coatings, and emulsion coatings. Water-soluble coatings are made from water and are environmentally friendly, offering easy application and quick drying. Solvent coatings are made from organic solvents and provide excellent durability and resistance to moisture. Emulsion coatings are a combination of water and synthetic resin, providing a high level of stain resistance and durability. These different types of coatings cater to various preferences and requirements of consumers in the interior wall coatings market.</p></p>
