<p><p>Intumescent Passive Fire Protection Coatings are specialized coatings that provide fire resistance to structural elements like steel and wood. <p><p>Intumescent passive fire protection coatings are used to enhance the fire resistance of structures. Water-based coatings are made with water as the main solvent, making them eco-friendly and easy to clean. They are commonly used in buildings where low VOC emissions are required.
