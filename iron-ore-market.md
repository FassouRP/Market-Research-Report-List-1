<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Iron ore is classified into different market types based on its physical characteristics and processing requirements. Sinter feed refers to iron ore fines that are used in the production of sinter, a material used in the blast furnace process. Pellet feed is smaller-sized iron ore fines that are converted into pellets, which are used as a raw material in electric arc furnaces. Lump iron ore consists of larger pieces and is often used in direct reduction processes. Pellet market refers to the global trade of iron ore pellets, which are used in steel production.</p></p>
