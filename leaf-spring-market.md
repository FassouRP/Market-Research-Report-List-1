<p><strong>The Leaf Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Leaf Spring</li><li>Mono-Leaf Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The leaf spring market includes two types: multi-leaf spring and mono-leaf spring. Multi-leaf springs are made up of multiple thin metal strips, or leaves, stacked together to create a flexible suspension system. They provide better load-carrying capacity and improved stability on rough terrains. On the other hand, mono-leaf springs consist of a single curved leaf and are known for their superior ride comfort and smoother operation. Both types cater to different requirements, providing varying levels of durability, load capacity, and ride quality.</p></p>
