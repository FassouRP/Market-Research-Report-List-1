Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS) utilizes hydraulic pressure to assist in steering, adding ease and control to the driver. Electronic Power Steering (EPS) uses an electric motor to provide steering assistance, enhancing fuel efficiency and responsiveness. Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS) combines the benefits of both HPS and EPS, utilizing hydraulic pressure assisted by an electric motor. These systems improve vehicle handling and maneuverability, ensuring a smooth and comfortable driving experience.</p></p>
