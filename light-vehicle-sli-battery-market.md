<p><p>The light vehicle SLI battery market can be categorized into three types: flooded type battery, maintenance-free type battery, and others. Flooded type batteries are the traditional type, consisting of removable caps for adding water and require regular maintenance. Maintenance-free type batteries, on the other hand, are sealed and do not require water addition or regular maintenance. They are more convenient but may have a shorter lifespan. The "others" category includes emerging battery technologies like advanced AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and gel batteries that have longer life and improved performance.</p></p>
