<p><p>The lightweight concrete wall panel market consists of three main types: EPS beads, ceramic beads, and foam concrete. EPS beads are small, lightweight balls made of expanded polystyrene. They are mixed with cement to create a lightweight and insulated wall panel. Ceramic beads, on the other hand, are small, lightweight spheres made of ceramic material. When mixed with cement, they provide better fire resistance and thermal insulation. Lastly, foam concrete is a mixture of cement, water, and foam agents, resulting in a lightweight and easily moldable wall panel.</p></p>
