<p><p>The liquid filter media market is categorized into four types. Mechanical filter media aid in removing solid particles from liquids through filtration mechanisms. Chemical filter media involve the use of activated carbon or ion-exchange resins to remove impurities through chemical reactions. Biological filter media utilize bacteria or other microorganisms to break down organic pollutants. Lastly, the "Others" category includes filter media with unique properties, such as absorbent materials. All these types of filter media play crucial roles in maintaining water purity in various industries.</p></p>
