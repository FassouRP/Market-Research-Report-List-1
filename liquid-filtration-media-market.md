<p><p>The liquid filtration media market comprises three main types: mechanical medias, chemical medias, and biological medias. Mechanical medias utilize physical mechanisms like sieve or barrier filtration to remove particles from liquids. Chemical medias involve the use of chemical substances such as activated carbon or ion exchange resins to capture and eliminate contaminants. Lastly, biological medias rely on living organisms like bacteria or fungi that break down organic matter and purify the liquid. These different types cater to specific filtration needs and are chosen based on the nature of contaminants and desired purification process.</p></p>
