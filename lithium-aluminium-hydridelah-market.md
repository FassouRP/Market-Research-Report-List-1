<p><p>Lithium Aluminium Hydride (LAH) is a compound used as a powerful reducing agent in various chemical reactions. The market for LAH is categorized into two types: the Schlesinger Method and the High-pressure Synthesis Method. The Schlesinger Method involves the reaction between lithium hydride and aluminium chloride in anhydrous ether solvent, producing LAH. This method is widely used due to its cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, the High-pressure Synthesis Method involves the reaction of lithium and aluminium in a high-pressure environment, resulting in LAH. This method is more efficient but requires specialized equipment.</p></p>
