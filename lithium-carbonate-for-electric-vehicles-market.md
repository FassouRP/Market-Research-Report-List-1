Salt lake extraction refers to the extraction of lithium from brines found in salt lakes, which often contain high concentrations of lithium. On the other hand, extraction of lithium ore involves mining lithium-containing minerals, such as spodumene or petalite, and processing them to obtain lithium carbonate.
