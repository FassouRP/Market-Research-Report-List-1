<p><p>The lithium solid-state battery market consists of two main types: polymer-based lithium solid-state batteries and lithium solid-state batteries with inorganic solid electrolytes. Polymer-based lithium solid-state batteries use polymer electrolytes to conduct lithium ions, making them flexible and lightweight. On the other hand, lithium solid-state batteries with inorganic solid electrolytes use solid ceramics as electrolytes, providing higher energy density and better stability. Both types aim to enhance the performance and safety of lithium-ion batteries by replacing liquid electrolytes with solid materials.</p></p>
