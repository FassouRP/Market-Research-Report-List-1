<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Track Brakes</li><li>Permanent Magnet Track Brakes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic track brakes are categorized into two market types: electromagnetic track brakes and permanent magnet track brakes. Electromagnetic track brakes utilize electromagnetic force to generate braking action, which can be controlled by adjusting the current. On the other hand, permanent magnet track brakes use permanent magnets to produce a magnetic field, which induces braking force upon the ferromagnetic track. Both types offer efficient and reliable braking solutions for a variety of applications, but the specific choice depends on factors such as power consumption, braking force, and control requirements.</p></p>
