<p><p>The marine engine lubricant market can be categorized into three main types: low-speed engine oil, middle-speed engine oil, and high-speed engine oil. Low-speed engine oil is specifically designed for large, slow-turning engines found in ships and tankers. Middle-speed engine oil is used for medium-sized engines, typically in ferries and fishing vessels. High-speed engine oil is utilized for small, high-performance engines present in speedboats and yachts. Each type of lubricant is formulated to meet the specific requirements and operating conditions of the respective engine speeds.</p></p>
