<p><p>The Marine Fire Extinguishing System market consists of different types of fire suppression technologies. Dry Powder fire extinguishing systems use dry chemical agents to suppress fires, which are effective in extinguishing Class A, B, C, and D fires. Bubble fire extinguishing systems are based on foam and gas mixtures, which suffocate the fire by smothering it. Other types of fire extinguishing systems may include water mist, carbon dioxide, or clean agent systems, each with their own unique properties and applications. These systems are designed specifically for use in marine environments to ensure the safety of onboard personnel and property.</p></p>
