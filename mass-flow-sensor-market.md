<p><p>The mass flow sensor market consists of various types such as hot wire air flow sensor, hot film air flow sensor, Karman vortex air flow sensor, and vane air flow sensor. Hot wire air flow sensors measure the amount of air passing through a wire, while hot film air flow sensors determine airflow by monitoring the change in resistance in a heated film. Karman vortex air flow sensors utilize the principle of vortex shedding to measure flow rate, and vane air flow sensors employ a rotating vane to measure the movement of air.</p></p>
