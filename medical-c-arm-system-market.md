<p><p>The Medical C-Arm System market consists of two major types: CCD Mobile C-Arm and FPD Mobile C-Arm. CCD Mobile C-Arm refers to the use of Charge-Coupled Device (CCD) technology in the imaging system, which provides high-quality images and is suitable for various medical procedures. On the other hand, FPD Mobile C-Arm uses Flat Panel Detector (FPD) technology, which offers improved image quality, higher resolution, and lower radiation dose compared to CCD. Both types are widely used in medical settings for diagnostic and interventional procedures.</p></p>
