<p><p>The Medical Micropump Market includes two main types: Gas Micropumps and Liquid Micropumps. Gas Micropumps are devices that generate gas flow by utilizing various principles like electrostatic forces or thermal gradients. They are used in medical applications such as drug delivery systems or gas analysis. On the other hand, Liquid Micropumps are designed to deliver precise amounts of liquid for medical purposes like drug administration or sample analysis. Both types of micropumps play crucial roles in the medical sector by providing efficient and controlled flow of either gas or liquid substances.</p></p>
