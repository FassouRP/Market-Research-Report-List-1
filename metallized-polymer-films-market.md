<p><p>Metallized polymer films are widely used in various industries due to their properties such as high barrier protection, heat resistance, and good printability. Metallized PET film refers to a polyester film that has been metallized with a thin layer of metal, typically aluminum, through a process called vapor deposition. This film is commonly used in packaging applications, providing excellent barrier properties and enhancing product shelf life. On the other hand, metallized OPP film refers to a polypropylene film that has undergone the same metallization process. It is commonly used in applications such as food packaging, decorative films, and labels, offering high gloss and improved aesthetics. Both types of films are popular choices for various industries seeking functional and visually appealing packaging solutions.</p></p>
