<p><p>Micro-hybrid vehicles, also known as start-stop vehicles, are automobiles that are equipped with an idle-stop system. This technology enables improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions by minimizing engine idling time.</p><p>The future outlook for the micro-hybrid vehicles market is promising.
