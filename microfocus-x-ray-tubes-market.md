<p><p>Microfocus X-ray tubes are specialized devices that generate and emit X-rays with high resolution and precision. There are two main types of X-ray tubes in the market: Open Tube and Sealed Tube. Open Tubes consist of a cathode and an anode, where electrons emitted from the cathode are accelerated towards the anode to produce X-rays. Sealed Tubes, on the other hand, are enclosed vacuum tubes where the cathode and anode are sealed within. Sealed Tubes provide longer lifespan and better stability than Open Tubes, making them suitable for various applications like medical imaging and industrial testing.</p></p>
