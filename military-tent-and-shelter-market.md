<p><p>The military tent and shelter market can be divided into two types: non-rigid and rigid. Non-rigid tents are flexible in structure and are typically made of lightweight materials such as nylon or canvas. These tents are easy to assemble and transport, making them suitable for temporary military operations. On the other hand, rigid shelters are more permanent structures, often constructed with heavy-duty materials like steel or aluminum. These shelters offer higher durability and can withstand extreme weather conditions, providing long-term accommodation for military personnel.</p></p>
