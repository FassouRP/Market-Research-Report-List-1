<p><ul><li>Wet Dust Suppressants</li><li>Dry Dust Suppressants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mining dust suppressants include two main types: wet dust suppressants and dry dust suppressants. Wet dust suppressants are typically water-based solutions that are sprayed onto mining surfaces to control dust particles. These suppressants act by absorbing moisture and weighing down the dust, preventing it from becoming airborne. On the other hand, dry dust suppressants use chemical compounds or additives that are applied to mining surfaces to prevent dust from becoming airborne. These compounds work by binding the dust particles together, forming a layer that suppresses dust dispersion.</p></p>
