<p><p>The Mobile C-arm X-ray Tubes Market includes two types of x-ray tubes: Stationary Anode and Rotating Anode. In the Stationary Anode market, the tube remains stationary during the imaging process, making it suitable for low-intensity applications. Conversely, in the Rotating Anode market, the tube rotates at high speeds to dissipate heat generated during the imaging process, making it ideal for high-intensity applications. Both types of tubes play a crucial role in the healthcare industry, providing portable and flexible imaging solutions for various medical procedures.</p></p>
