<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Molded goods market types include Liquid Injection Molding (LIM) and Liquid Silicon Rubber (LSR). LIM involves injecting liquid material into a mold, which is then cured to form the final product. LIM is commonly used to create complex and intricate parts that require high precision. On the other hand, LSR involves injecting liquid silicone rubber into a mold, which is then heat-cured to form the desired product. LSR offers excellent chemical resistance, durability, and flexibility, making it ideal for applications in industries like healthcare and automotive.</p></p>
