<p><p>A Motorcycle Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) is a safety feature that prevents the wheels from locking up while braking, thus enabling the rider to maintain control of the motorcycle. It is designed to decrease the braking distance and minimize the risk of skidding and accidents, especially in wet or slippery road conditions. ABS systems for motorcycles typically involve sensors that measure the rotational speed of the wheels and control units that modulate the brake pressure to prevent lockups.</p><p>The Motorcycle Anti-Lock Braking System Market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue to grow at a CAGR of 14% during the forecast period.
