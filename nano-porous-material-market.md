<p><p>Nano-porous materials refer to materials that have a high density of pores at the nano-scale level. The market for nano-porous materials can be classified into various types, including zeolites, silica gel, activated carbon, activated alumina, and others. Zeolites are crystalline materials with well-defined pores, silica gel is a synthetic amorphous form of silicon dioxide, activated carbon is a highly porous carbon material, activated alumina is a porous form of aluminum oxide, and "others" include nano-porous materials that do not fall under the other categories. These materials are used in various applications such as adsorption, separation, catalysis, and filtration.</p></p>
