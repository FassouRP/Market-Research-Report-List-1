<p><p>Nonre-circulating air curtains are a type of device used to create a barrier of air across door openings, preventing the exchange of indoor and outdoor air. There are three common types of installations for these air curtains: horizontal installation, vertical installation, and recessed market. </p><p>Horizontal installation involves mounting the air curtain above the doorway, blowing the air horizontally across the opening. Vertical installation, on the other hand, requires mounting the air curtain on one side of the doorway, with the air blowing vertically down the opening. Lastly, recessed market air curtains are designed to be installed within the ceiling, providing an invisible barrier of air without obstructing the doorway.</p></p>
