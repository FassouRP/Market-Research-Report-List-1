<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Fiber Splitters market can be divided into two types, Type I and Type II. Type I market refers to the segment where the splitters are used in FTTx (Fiber to the x) networks, including applications like Fiber to the Home (FTTH) and Fiber to the Premises (FTTP). These splitters typically have lower splitting ratios and are widely used for residential and small business connections. Type II market, on the other hand, involves splitters used in long-haul and metropolitan area networks (MANs), which require higher splitting ratios to accommodate larger bandwidth requirements in commercial settings.</p></p>
