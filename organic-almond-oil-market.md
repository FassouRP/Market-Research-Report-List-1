<p><p>Organic almond oil is classified into two main types: sweet almond oil and bitter almond oil. Sweet almond oil is derived from the kernels of sweet almonds and is widely used in skincare and haircare products due to its moisturizing and nourishing properties. It is also commonly used in culinary applications. On the other hand, bitter almond oil is extracted from the bitter almond tree and is primarily used for its fragrance in perfumes and cosmetic products. However, it is important to note that bitter almond oil contains amygdalin, a toxic compound, and should not be ingested.</p></p>
