Lateral Flow Assay Test Kits are the most common type, where a sample is applied to a test strip and results are obtained through visible color changes. Dipstick Test Kits involve immersing a test strip into a sample and observing color changes. Microfluidic Paper-based Systems utilize small channels and chambers in paper to perform complex diagnostic tests. These kits are cost-effective, rapid, and can be used in remote or resource-limited settings.</p></p>
