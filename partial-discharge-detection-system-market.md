<p><p>The Partial Discharge Detection System Market includes two types of detection methods: online detection and offline detection. Online detection refers to the continuous monitoring of partial discharges in electrical equipment while it is in operation. This method allows for real-time analysis and helps in assessing the health of the equipment. On the other hand, offline detection involves periodically testing the electrical equipment for partial discharges when it is not in operation. This method helps in assessing the condition and detecting any potential issues before the equipment is put back into operation.</p></p>
