<p><p>The passenger vehicle HVAC market consists of two types: Manual HVAC and Automatic HVAC. Manual HVAC systems require the driver or passengers to manually adjust the temperature, fan speed, and mode settings. These systems typically come with basic controls and knobs. On the other hand, Automatic HVAC systems use sensors and electronic controls to automatically regulate the temperature, fan speed, and air distribution within the vehicle. These systems offer more convenience and precision in maintaining desired comfort levels without relying on manual adjustments.</p></p>
