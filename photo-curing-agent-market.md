<p><p>The photo curing agent market can be classified into two types: free-radical type and cationic type. Free-radical type agents initiate a chemical reaction when exposed to light, creating a strong bond between molecules. This type is commonly used in UV curable coatings, adhesives, and inks. On the other hand, cationic type agents use light to activate a reaction that generates cations, resulting in the bonding of polymers. This type finds applications in electronic devices, automotive parts, and medical equipment manufacturing. Both types offer efficient and reliable curing solutions for various industries.</p></p>
