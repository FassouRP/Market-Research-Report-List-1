<p><p>Photoacid generator (PAGs) is a compound used in the production of various products like coatings, adhesives, and printing inks. PAGs can be categorized into three types in relation to their market usage. Firstly, photoinitiators are PAGs that generate acids upon exposure to UV light, triggering the curing process of coatings and inks. Secondly, UV absorbing agents are PAGs that absorb and dissipate UV light, thus protecting the substrate from UV-induced degradation. Lastly, there are other markets where PAGs find applications, such as in semiconductor manufacturing, lithography, and pharmaceutical industries. These markets utilize PAGs for their unique chemical properties and functionalities.</p></p>
