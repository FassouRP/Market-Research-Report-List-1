<p><p>Piezoceramic composites are advanced materials that are composed of both piezoelectric ceramics and other materials, such as polymers or metals. Due to these properties, piezoceramic composites are widely used in various applications, including sensors, actuators, transducers, and energy harvesting devices.</p><p>The future outlook for the piezoceramic composites market is positive and shows significant growth potential. The increasing demand for energy-efficient and compact electronic devices, such as smartphones, wearables, and medical implants, is driving the market growth.
