<p><p>Plasma surface treatment equipment refers to the machines used to enhance the surface properties of materials through plasma treatment. There are two main types: low pressure/vacuum plasma surface treatment equipment and atmospheric plasma surface treatment equipment. </p><p>- Low pressure/vacuum plasma equipment operates in a controlled, low-pressure environment, making it suitable for delicate or heat-sensitive materials. It provides a more uniform and precise treatment.</p><p>- Atmospheric plasma equipment, on the other hand, operates at atmospheric pressure, allowing for faster treatment and larger-scale applications. It is commonly used in industries such as automotive and electronics for surface cleaning, activation, and deposition processes.</p></p>
