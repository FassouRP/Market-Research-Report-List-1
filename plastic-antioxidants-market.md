<p><p>The plastic antioxidants market can be categorized into different types. Phenolic antioxidants are additives used to prevent oxidation and degradation of plastic materials. Phosphite and phosphonite antioxidants function by inhibiting the oxidation process. Antioxidant blends are mixtures of different types of antioxidants used to enhance their effectiveness. Lastly, other antioxidants refer to various types that do not fall into the aforementioned categories. These different types of plastic antioxidants serve the purpose of protecting plastic materials from degradation caused by oxidation, thus extending their lifespan.</p></p>

<p><p>Plastic antioxidants find application in both thermoplastic and thermosetting plastics. In thermoplastic plastics, antioxidants are incorporated to prevent degradation and maintain their stability during processing and use. They help reduce discoloration, maintain mechanical properties, and extend the lifespan of the plastic. In thermosetting plastics, antioxidants play a crucial role in preventing the premature curing or aging of the material. They ensure the integrity and performance of the plastic by protecting it from oxidation and degradation caused by heat and light exposure.</p></p>
