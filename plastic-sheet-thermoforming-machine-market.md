<p><p>Plastic sheet thermoforming machines are used to shape plastic sheets into desired forms. The market is divided into two types: Single Station Thermoforming Machine and Multi Station Thermoforming Machine. Single Station machines have a single station where the heating, forming, and cooling processes take place sequentially. They are simpler and used for low-volume production. Multi Station machines have multiple stations that allow simultaneous heating, forming, and cooling of multiple plastic sheets. They are more complex and used for high-volume production. These machines enable efficient and flexible plastic sheet manufacturing processes.</p></p>
