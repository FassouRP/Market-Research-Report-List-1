<p><p>PM2.5 sensor market types encompass infrared sensors and laser sensors. Infrared sensors utilize light absorption properties to detect PM2.5 particles, allowing for accurate measurements. This type of sensor is commonly used in indoor air quality monitoring systems. On the other hand, laser sensors operate by scattering laser beams off particles and analyzing the scattered light to determine PM2.5 concentration. This type of sensor offers high precision and is often found in air pollution monitoring devices. Both infrared and laser sensors play crucial roles in providing reliable and precise data for PM2.5 monitoring applications.</p></p>
