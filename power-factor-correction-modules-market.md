<p><p>Power Factor Correction Modules (PFCMs) are electronic devices used to optimize the power factor in electrical systems. There are different types of PFCMs available in the market, including power capacitors, AC reactors, active power filters, and others. Power capacitors are used to store and release electrical energy to improve power factor. AC reactors reduce harmonics and improve the efficiency of electrical systems. Active power filters actively control and correct the power factor. Other types of PFCMs include static VAR compensators and thyristor-switched capacitors, which all aim to improve overall power factor and energy efficiency in electrical systems.</p></p>
