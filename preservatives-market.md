<p><p>Preservatives play a vital role in preventing the spoilage of various products. Organic solvent preservatives are chemical compounds that dissolve in organic solvents, effectively preventing the growth of microorganisms. Inorganic preservatives, on the other hand, rely on minerals or metallic compounds to inhibit microbial growth. Natural antiseptics are derived from plant extracts and possess antimicrobial properties. Ester-type preservatives are organic compounds characterized by ester bonds, commonly used in cosmetics and personal care products due to their ability to inhibit the growth of bacteria and fungi.</p></p>
