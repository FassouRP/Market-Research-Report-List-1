<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The PVC leather market consists of two types: Calender PVC Leather and Rotary Screen Coating PVC Leather. Calender PVC Leather is produced through a process called calendaring, where PVC material is combined with a fabric base using heat and pressure. This gives the leather a smooth and uniform appearance. On the other hand, Rotary Screen Coating PVC Leather involves applying PVC material directly onto a fabric base using a rotary screen. This method creates a textured and patterned leather finish. Both types of PVC leather have important applications in various industries.</p></p>
