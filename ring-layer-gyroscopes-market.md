<p><p>Ring layer gyroscopes are advanced sensors used for measuring angular velocity and rotational movements. They are categorized into different types based on the number of axes they can measure. 1-axis gyroscopes measure rotation in only one plane, typically the yaw axis. 2-axis gyroscopes measure rotation in two planes, usually yaw and pitch. 3-axis gyroscopes are capable of measuring rotation in all three axes of yaw, pitch, and roll. Additionally, there are other specialized types of gyroscopes available for specific applications, such as hemispherical resonator gyroscopes or optical gyroscopes. These variations provide various options to meet different measurement needs in industries like aerospace, automotive, and robotics.</p></p>
