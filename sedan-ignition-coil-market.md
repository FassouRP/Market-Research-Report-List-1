Single-spark ignition coils are designed to generate a single electrical spark per ignition cycle, providing a traditional and reliable method. On the other hand, Multi-spark ignition coils generate multiple sparks per ignition cycle, improving fuel efficiency and enhancing the performance of the engine. Both types have their advantages and cater to different customer needs, ensuring a diverse and competitive Sedan Ignition Coil market.</p></p>
