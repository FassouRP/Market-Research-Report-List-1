<p><p>Seed binders are substances used to bind seed coatings together, allowing them to adhere to the seeds during planting. Polyvinyl alcohol binders and polymer binders are two types of seed binders commonly used in the market. Polyvinyl alcohol binders are water-soluble and provide strong seed adhesion, ensuring effective planting and germination. Polymer binders, on the other hand, are synthetic materials that offer higher flexibility, durability, and resistance to environmental factors, enhancing seed coating integrity. Both types of binders play a crucial role in improving seed performance and ensuring successful plant growth.</p></p>
