<p><p>A self-supporting run-flat tire is a type of tire that can continue to operate even when punctured or deflated, allowing the vehicle to be driven at a reduced speed for a limited distance. Self-supporting run-flat tires offer drivers added safety and convenience as they eliminate the need for immediate replacement or repair in the event of a puncture.</p><p>The self-supporting run-flat tire market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period.
