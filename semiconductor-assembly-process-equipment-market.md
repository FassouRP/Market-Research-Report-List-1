<p><p>The semiconductor assembly process equipment market can be categorized into four types. Die bonders are used to attach integrated circuits onto a substrate. Wire bonders connect the integrated circuit to the package or substrate using thin wires. Packaging equipment involves processes like encapsulation, sealing, and testing of the assembled devices. Lastly, the "Others" category includes equipment used for processes such as wafer level packaging, flip-chip bonding, and die sorting. These equipment types play crucial roles in the manufacturing and packaging of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
