<p><p>Servo brakes are a type of braking system used in vehicles to enhance their braking performance. There are three main types of servo brakes on the market, namely vacuum servo brakes, pneumatic servo brakes, and hydraulic servo brakes. Vacuum servo brakes rely on vacuum pressure to assist in brake application, while pneumatic servo brakes use compressed air. Hydraulic servo brakes, on the other hand, employ hydraulic pressure to enhance braking force. Each type of servo brake offers different advantages and is suited for specific applications based on the vehicle's requirements and design.</p></p>
