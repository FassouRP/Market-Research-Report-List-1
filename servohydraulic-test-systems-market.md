<p><p>Servohydraulic Test Systems can be categorized into two main types: Dynamic Cyclic Testing and Static Testing. </p><p>Dynamic Cyclic Testing involves subjecting a test specimen to repeated cycles of loading, simulating real-life conditions. This type of testing is commonly used in fatigue and durability evaluations. </p><p>On the other hand, Static Testing involves applying a constant load or force to a test specimen without any cyclic variations. This type of testing is typically used to determine the strength, stiffness, and deformation characteristics of a material or component under static or non-changing conditions.</p></p>
