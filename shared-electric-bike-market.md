<p><p>The shared electric bike market can be divided into two types, namely point-to-point and distributed markets. In a point-to-point market, users can pick up and drop off the bikes at any designated location. This provides more flexibility in terms of trip planning and destination. On the other hand, in a distributed market, the bikes are available at fixed docking stations or designated areas throughout the city. Users need to pick up and drop off the bikes at these specific locations, offering a more organized and controlled system for bike sharing.</p></p>
