AIS helps in preventing collisions, enhancing navigational safety, and improving maritime domain awareness.</p><p>The Shipborne Automatic Identification System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.7% during the forecast period. <p><ul><li>Class A</li><li>Class B</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Shipborne Automatic Identification System (AIS) market includes two main types: Class A and Class B. Class A AIS systems are mandatory for all large and commercial vessels, providing accurate real-time information about vessel identity, position, course, and speed. These systems are designed to meet strict international regulations and ensure the safety and efficiency of maritime operations. On the other hand, Class B AIS systems are designed for smaller vessels and leisure crafts, offering limited functionality compared to Class A. These systems aim to enhance situational awareness and promote collision avoidance for different types of vessels.</p></p>
