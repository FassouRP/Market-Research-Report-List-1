<p><p>Shrimp grading machines are available in two types: multi-function and single-function. Multi-function machines possess multiple capabilities, allowing them to perform various tasks such as sorting, grading, and sizing shrimps. These versatile machines offer convenience and efficiency as they can handle different aspects of shrimp processing. On the other hand, single-function machines are specialized for a specific task, typically grading shrimps based on size or quality. While they lack the versatility of multi-function machines, single-function machines are preferred when there is a specific need for grading shrimps only.</p></p>
