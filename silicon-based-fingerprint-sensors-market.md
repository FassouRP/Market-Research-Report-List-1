<p><p>Silicon-based fingerprint sensors are a type of biometric technology used in various applications for identity authentication. Swipe sensors are the more traditional type, consisting of a thin strip that users swipe their fingers across. Area sensors, on the other hand, are larger and offer a flat surface for users to place their entire finger onto. Both types capture the unique pattern of ridges and valleys on the fingertip, converting it into a digital image for comparison and verification purposes. The market for silicon-based fingerprint sensors includes a range of industries such as smartphones, laptops, access control systems, and payment cards.</p></p>
