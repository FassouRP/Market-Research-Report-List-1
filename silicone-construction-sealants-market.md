<p><p>Silicone construction sealants are widely used in the construction industry for sealing joints and gaps to prevent leakage and ensure structural integrity. One-component sealants are ready to use and do not require any additional mixing or curing agents. In contrast, two-component sealants consist of a base material and a curing agent which need to be mixed before application. These types provide different advantages and properties, such as quick curing time, flexibility, and high strength, catering to the diverse needs of the construction sector.</p></p>
