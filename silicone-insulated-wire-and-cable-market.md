<p><p>Silicone Insulated Wire and Cable market can be categorized into two types: Single Core and Multi-core. Single core cables consist of a single conductor covered with silicone insulation, generally used for applications that require high temperatures, flexibility, and resistance to chemicals. On the other hand, multi-core cables have multiple conductors with individual silicone insulation, making them suitable for more complex wiring configurations. Both types offer excellent thermal stability, electrical performance, and durability, making them widely used in various industries like automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
