<p><p>Single Beam Side Scan Sonar (SBSS) is a type of sonar system used for underwater imaging and mapping. It is commonly used in marine exploration, hydrographic surveys, and underwater archaeology. Unlike multibeam sonar, which uses multiple transducers to image a wide swath of the seafloor, SBSS uses a single transducer to generate a narrow beam of sound that sweeps across the seafloor. The returning echoes are then processed to create a detailed image of the seabed.</p><p>The Single Beam Side Scan Sonar Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, there is an increasing demand for accurate and detailed underwater mapping for a variety of applications such as oil and gas exploration, marine construction, and environmental monitoring. SBSS provides high-resolution imaging capabilities that are essential for these applications.</p><p>Furthermore, technological advancements in SBSS systems are driving market growth. Manufacturers are continuously improving the performance and reliability of SBSS systems, resulting in higher quality images and increased efficiency in data collection.
