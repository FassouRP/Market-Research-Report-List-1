<p><p>The ski wax market can be categorized into two main types: glide waxes and grip waxes. Glide waxes are used to reduce friction between the skis and the snow, improving the overall speed and maneuverability. They are typically applied to the entire length of the ski base. Grip waxes, on the other hand, are designed to provide traction and prevent skis from slipping during uphill climbs or on icy surfaces. These waxes are typically applied to specific areas of the ski, such as the kick zone, to enhance grip and control.</p></p>
