<p><p>Smart glass for the automotive market refers to the use of advanced glass technologies that offer various functionalities. Electrochromic glass can change its tint level, providing control over light and heat passing through the windows. Suspended Particle Display (SPD) technology uses a film containing suspended particles to control the amount of light transmitted. Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC) technology uses a film with liquid crystals dispersed in a polymer matrix to switch between transparent and opaque states. These technologies improve comfort, privacy, and energy efficiency in vehicles.</p></p>
