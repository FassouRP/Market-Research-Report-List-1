<p><p>Smart glass in the automotive market is classified into two types: active smart windows and passive smart windows. Active smart windows can change their light transmission properties with the use of electricity, allowing drivers to control the amount of light and heat entering the vehicle. On the other hand, passive smart windows are designed to automatically regulate the amount of light and heat passing through, without any electric operation. Both types of smart windows provide benefits such as enhanced comfort, improved energy efficiency, and increased privacy for passengers.</p></p>
