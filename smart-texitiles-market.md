<p><p>Smart textiles, also known as electronic textiles or e-textiles, refer to fabrics that integrate electronic elements and technologies to provide additional functionalities. Advancements in materials science and electronics have led to the development of more sophisticated and flexible electronic components, enabling the integration of smart functionalities into textiles seamlessly.</p><p>The COVID-19 pandemic has also highlighted the importance of smart textiles in healthcare. These textiles can monitor vital signs, detect infections, and provide real-time information, thereby contributing to the early detection and prevention of diseases. As a result, there has been a surge in the adoption of smart textiles in the medical sector, driving the market's growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing interest in fitness tracking and connected devices has fueled the demand for smart textiles in the sports and fitness industry.
