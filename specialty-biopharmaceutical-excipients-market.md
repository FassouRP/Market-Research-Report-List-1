<p><p>Specialty biopharmaceutical excipients are additives used in the formulation of biopharmaceutical drugs to enhance their performance and stability. There are several types of excipients available in the market. Firstly, "膨胀剂" or disintegrants aid in the rapid breakup of tablets or capsules in the gastrointestinal tract for improved drug absorption. Secondly, "溶解度增强剂" or solubility enhancers improve the drug's solubility, allowing for better dissolution and absorption. Thirdly, "表面活性剂" or surfactants improve drug compatibility and stability. Lastly, the "其他" or other category includes various excipients used for specific purposes like viscosity modification or taste masking.</p></p>
