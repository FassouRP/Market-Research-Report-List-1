<p><p>The Spinal Fusion Biologics market encompasses various types, including Bone Graft, Bone Graft Substitute, Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP), and Other. Bone graft involves using grafts from the patient's own body or an allograft to stimulate bone growth. Bone graft substitutes are synthetic materials that promote bone fusion. Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) involves using the patient's own blood to concentrate platelets, which contain growth factors that aid in the healing process. The "Other" category may include emerging biologic products that have not yet been widely adopted in spinal fusion procedures.</p></p>
