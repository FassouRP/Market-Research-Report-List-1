<p><p>Sport-fishing motor-yachts are divided into two main market types based on their hull design: monohull and multihull. They are the more traditional type of motor-yachts and are typically preferred for their efficient performance. On the other hand, multihull motor-yachts have two parallel hulls connected by a deck, offering increased stability and deck space. They are becoming popular in the sport-fishing market due to their comfortable and spacious layout, allowing for a luxurious fishing experience.</p></p>
