<p><p>Sport Utility Vehicles (SUVs) come in two market types: Two-wheel Drive and Four-wheel Drive. Two-wheel Drive SUVs are designed with power delivery to either the front or rear wheels, providing better fuel efficiency on regular roads. They are suitable for urban driving and moderate off-road situations. Four-wheel Drive SUVs, on the other hand, have power delivered to all four wheels, offering enhanced traction and maneuverability. They are popular for more challenging terrains and outdoor activities, providing better capability in snow, mud, or uneven ground conditions.</p></p>
