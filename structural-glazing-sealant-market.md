<p><p>The structural glazing sealant market is categorized into different types based on the resin used. These include epoxy, polyurethane, acrylic, methyl methacrylate, cyanoacrylate, and others. Epoxy resin sealants offer high strength and durability, making them suitable for bonding various substrates. Polyurethane sealants provide excellent adhesion and elasticity, making them ideal for sealing joints subjected to movement. Acrylic sealants are known for their weather resistance and flexibility. Methyl methacrylate sealants offer high bond strength and fast curing properties. Cyanoacrylate sealants are quick-drying and provide strong adhesion. Other types of sealants may include silicone, hybrid, or solvent-based formulations.</p></p>
