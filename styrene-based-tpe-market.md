It consists of different types: 1) SBS is a general-purpose TPE with excellent flexibility and processability. 2) SIS offers superior clarity and adhesion, making it suitable for applications like hot-melt adhesives. 3) SEBS combines the properties of SBS and SIS, providing enhanced resistance to UV radiation and high temperatures. 4) SEPS has better oil resistance and is commonly used in automotive applications. 5) Compound type is a blend of TPE with other polymers for specialized applications. There are also other niche markets for Styrene-based TPE, depending on specific requirements and industries.</p></p>
