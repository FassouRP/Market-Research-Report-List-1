Semi-synthetic gear oil, on the other hand, is a blend of synthetic and mineral oil. It combines the enhanced properties of synthetic oil with the cost-effectiveness of mineral oil. Semi-synthetic oil is commonly used in a wide range of industrial and automotive gear applications where moderate performance is required.</p></p>
