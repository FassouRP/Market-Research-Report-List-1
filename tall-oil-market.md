<p><p>Tall oil is a byproduct of the kraft pulping process in the paper industry. There are three main types of tall oil: Softwood Tall Oil, Mixed Tall Oil, and Hardwood Tall Oil. Softwood Tall Oil is derived from softwood trees, such as pine, and is high in fatty acids. Mixed Tall Oil is a blend of both softwood and hardwood tall oil and contains a balanced combination of fatty acids and resin acids. Hardwood Tall Oil is obtained from hardwood trees and has a higher content of resin acids. These different types of tall oil cater to various industries and applications.</p></p>
