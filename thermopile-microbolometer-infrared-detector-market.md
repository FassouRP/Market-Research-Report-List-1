<p><p>The Thermopile Microbolometer Infrared Detector market consists of three main types of infrared detectors: Microbolometer IR Detector, Thermopile IR Detector, and Pyroelectric IR Detector. The Microbolometer IR Detector is a thermal detector that measures infrared radiation through temperature changes. The Thermopile IR Detector relies on the Seebeck effect to convert temperature differences into electrical signals. Lastly, the Pyroelectric IR Detector generates an electrical pulse when exposed to infrared radiation due to the pyroelectric effect. All three types play crucial roles in various applications such as surveillance systems, temperature measurement, and medical imaging.</p></p>
