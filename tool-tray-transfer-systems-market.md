<p><p>Tool tray transfer systems consist of trays or pallets that are designed to hold and transport tools and other materials within a manufacturing or assembly line. These systems are categorized into two types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. </p><p>Fully automatic tool tray transfer systems are equipped with advanced robotic technology, allowing for automated loading, unloading, and transportation of the trays. These systems require minimal human intervention and are ideal for high-volume production lines.</p><p>On the other hand, semi-automatic tool tray transfer systems involve a combination of manual and automated operations. While the loading and unloading of trays may require human intervention, the transportation of the trays is automated. These systems are suited for medium-volume production lines where some level of human control is desired.</p></p>
