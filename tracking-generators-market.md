<p><p>A tracking generator is a test instrument used in electronics and telecommunications to generate a swept frequency output signal. The output signal of a tracking generator is swept over a frequency range, and the signal response is observed and analyzed by the spectrum analyzer.</p><p>The current outlook of the tracking generators market is positive, and the future also appears promising.
