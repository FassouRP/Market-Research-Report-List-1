<p><p>The UHMWPE market can be categorized into two types: powder UHMWPE and particle UHMWPE. Powder UHMWPE refers to the finely ground form of the material, which can be easily incorporated into various applications such as coatings, adhesives, and composites. On the other hand, particle UHMWPE is characterized by larger-sized particles, generally used for products like sheets, rods, and tubes. While powder UHMWPE offers versatility and ease of use for different manufacturing processes, particle UHMWPE provides greater strength and durability for structural applications.</p></p>
