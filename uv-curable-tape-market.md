<p><p>The UV curable tape market can be categorized into two types: single-side tape and double-side tape. Single-side tape refers to a type of tape that has adhesive applied on only one side, allowing it to stick to surfaces. It is commonly used for bonding applications where one-sided adhesion is required. On the other hand, double-side tape is a type of tape with adhesive applied on both sides. This type of tape is popular for joining two surfaces together and is commonly used in industries such as construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
