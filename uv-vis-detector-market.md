<p><p>The UV-Vis detector market is segmented into different types, including PDA (photodiode array), RI (refractive index), and others. PDA detectors utilize an array of photodiodes to detect and measure light intensity at different wavelengths, providing a detailed spectral analysis. RI detectors, on the other hand, measure refractive index changes in a sample to determine concentration or purity. The "Others" category encompasses various detectors like fluorescence detectors, electrochemical detectors, and more, each designed for specific analytical applications or detection requirements in the UV-Vis spectroscopy field.</p></p>
