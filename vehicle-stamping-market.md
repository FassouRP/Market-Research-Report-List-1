<p><p>The vehicle stamping market consists of two main types: hot stamping and cold stamping. Hot stamping involves heating the metal sheet and then rapidly cooling it to increase its strength and formability. This method is commonly used for creating complex and lightweight vehicle parts. On the other hand, cold stamping involves shaping the metal sheet at room temperature, making it suitable for simpler vehicle components. Both hot and cold stamping play crucial roles in manufacturing various parts, allowing vehicles to have sturdy structures and efficient designs.</p></p>
