<p><p>The Video Image Smoke Detection (VISD) System market can be divided into two types: active detection systems and passive detection systems. Active detection systems use lasers or infrared beams to actively monitor and detect smoke particles. These systems provide early detection and can quickly alert authorities. On the other hand, passive detection systems rely on existing surveillance cameras to analyze video images and identify smoke patterns. While they may not be as proactive as active systems, passive systems can still effectively detect and alert authorities in case of smoke presence.</p></p>
