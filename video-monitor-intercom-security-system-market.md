<p><p>The video monitor intercom security system market consists of two main types: analog type and IP type. Analog video monitor intercom systems use traditional wiring to transmit audio and video signals, making them suitable for older buildings. IP video monitor intercom systems, on the other hand, utilize internet protocol technology to transmit signals through an existing network, offering more flexibility and scalability. While analog systems are often more affordable, IP systems provide higher resolution and advanced features like remote access and integration with other security devices.</p></p>
