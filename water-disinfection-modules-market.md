<p><p>The water disinfection modules market consists of three main types: water flow type, water tank type, and other markets. </p><p>Water flow type modules are designed to treat water as it flows through a system, ensuring continuous disinfection. These modules are commonly used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and wastewater treatment.</p><p>Water tank type modules, on the other hand, are installed in water storage tanks and work by disinfecting the entire water volume within the tank. They are suitable for applications where water is stored for an extended period, such as residential and commercial buildings.</p><p>The "other" market category includes various types of modules that are not specifically classified under water flow or water tank types.
