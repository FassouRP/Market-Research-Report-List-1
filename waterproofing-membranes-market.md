<p><p>Waterproofing membranes are materials used to prevent the ingress of water and moisture into various structures. Sheet-based membranes are pre-fabricated membranes made of rubber, bitumen, or plastic, commonly used for roofing and underground applications. They are cost-effective and easy to install. Liquid-applied membranes, on the other hand, are made of liquid polymers that can be directly applied on surfaces using techniques like spraying or rolling. They provide a seamless and flexible barrier against water and are suitable for complex and irregular structures. Both types offer effective waterproofing solutions for different construction needs.</p></p>
