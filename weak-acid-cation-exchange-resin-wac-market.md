<p><p>The Weak Acid Cation Exchange Resin (WAC) market comprises two main types: Gel Weak Acid Cation Exchange Resin (WAC) and Macroporous Weak Acid Cation Exchange Resin (WAC). Gel WAC is a solid, gel-like resin used in various industries for removing impurities from water, such as heavy metals and organic compounds. Both types play a crucial role in purifying water by selectively exchanging cations for contaminant removal.</p></p>
