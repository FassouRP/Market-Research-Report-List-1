<p><p>Wheelchairs are assistive devices designed to aid individuals with mobility impairments. Powered wheelchairs are electrically driven, offering motorized movement through controls, making them suitable for people with significant physical limitations. They provide independence and convenience. Manual wheelchairs, on the other hand, require the user to self-propel using their upper body strength. They are simpler in design, lighter in weight, and more affordable compared to powered wheelchairs. Manual wheelchairs are preferred by individuals with sufficient upper body strength or those who require less assistance for mobility. Both types serve the purpose of improving mobility and enhancing the quality of life for individuals with disabilities.</p></p>
