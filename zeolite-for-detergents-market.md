<p><strong>The Zeolite for Detergents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Zeolite A</li><li>Zeolite P</li><li>Zeolite X and AX</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Zeolites are crystalline compounds widely used in detergents due to their impressive adsorption properties. The zeolite detergent market can be categorized into three main types: Zeolite A, Zeolite P, and Zeolite X. Zeolite A has a high calcium binding capacity, making it effective in removing hardness ions from water. Zeolite P, on the other hand, has a remarkable adsorption capacity for anionic surfactants, aiding in stain removal. Lastly, Zeolite X has a balanced ability to remove both hardness ions and anionic surfactants, making it versatile and suitable for various detergent formulations. The AX market refers to detergent formulations that combine both Zeolite A and Zeolite X for enhanced performance.</p></p>
